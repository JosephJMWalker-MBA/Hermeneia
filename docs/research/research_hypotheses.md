## RH-001 — Disciplined Revision as the Primitive Operation

**Recorded:** 2026-06-25

### Hypothesis

Disciplined revision is the primitive operation from which the evolution of understanding emerges.

Formally:

> U(n+1) = R(U(n), E, Δ)

where:
- U(n) = current understanding
- E = available evidence
- Δ = revision introduced through investigation
- R = disciplined revision process

The simpler form: `Understanding(n+1) = Understanding(n) + Disciplined Revision`

The formal form matters because R is a function, not an additive term. New evidence may reinforce understanding, overturn it, or merely clarify it. The relationship is not always additive.

### Claim

If this is true, then:
- Evolution is accumulated revision under evidence
- Every component of Hermeneia exists to govern one aspect of R
- Explorer generates candidate revisions
- Architect organizes revisions
- Artist communicates revisions
- Critic evaluates revisions
- Steward ratifies revisions
- Witness (if it survives research) observes whether revisions reached another investigator

The universal operation is not "understanding." It is revision. Understanding is what is being revised.

### Confidence

**Low.**

### Supporting Evidence

- Gatsby Experiment 001 (Epistemic framework)
- Gatsby Experiment 002 (Historical-Relational framework)
- Gatsby Experiment 003 (Processual-Adaptive framework)
- The Architecture Freeze: a period of intentional stability that allowed methodological observations to emerge from repeated execution — itself a demonstration that constrained revision produces legible patterns

### Counter-Evidence

None yet sought.

### Competing Explanations

- Understanding evolves through accumulation rather than revision (additive model)
- The primitive operation is attention, not revision
- The framework is domain-specific to literary analysis and does not generalize

### Next Experiment

Genesis. A new corpus in a different domain (theology, law, scientific literature) should be investigated under the same pipeline to determine whether the process model observed in the Gatsby experiments replicates.

## RH-002 — The Architecture Freeze as an Epistemological Instrument

**Recorded:** 2026-06-25

### Hypothesis

Intentional architectural stability is a prerequisite for methodology discovery. Without it, patterns produced by the system are confounded with patterns produced by changes to the system.

### Claim

The Architecture Freeze v1.0 was not primarily about preventing changes. It was about creating the conditions under which an epistemology could emerge from repeated execution.

Scientific instruments work the same way: the instrument must be stable before the observations it produces can be interpreted.

### Confidence

**Low-Medium.** This is supported by the trajectory of the Hermeneia research program — the discoveries that appeared during the freeze (Explorer, Blueprint as Intent Hypothesis, calibration, question selection, priority functions, constitutional literature) would have been difficult to distinguish from architectural artifacts if the architecture had continued changing.

### Supporting Evidence

- Architecture Freeze v1.0 (2026-05-XX to 2026-06-25)
- Concepts discovered during freeze: Explorer, Blueprint as Intent Hypothesis, calibration vs. validation distinction, question selection as primary divergence driver, constitutional literature, investigative framework as primary variable

### Counter-Evidence

None yet sought. The alternative explanation — that these discoveries would have emerged regardless — has not been tested.

### Competing Explanations

- The freeze was coincidental; the discoveries would have emerged regardless
- The freeze delayed productive architectural improvements

### Next Experiment

Comparative case study: identify another AI research project that underwent a period of intentional architectural stability and examine whether similar pattern-emergence occurred. (Long-range research; not currently scoped.)
