## Purpose

This document consolidates findings across three executions of the Hermeneia pipeline applied to the same corpus in three culturally and linguistically distinct contexts. Its purpose is to determine which findings are stable across executions, which diverge predictably, and what the pattern of stability and divergence implies.

The prior two-experiment analysis (`experiment_001_002_comparative_analysis.md`) produced several hypotheses. Experiment 003 (Mandarin) provides a third data point against which to evaluate them.

## Layer-by-Layer Comparison

### Layer 1: Observation

**Stable across all three executions.**

All three investigators identified the same major textual phenomena:

- Green light (绿灯 / la luz verde / the green light)
- Water / Bay (水域 / el agua / the bay)
- Clock (钟表 / el reloj / the clock)
- Valley of Ashes (灰烬谷 / el valle de cenizas / the valley of ashes)
- Road / Cars (道路 / la carretera / the road)
- Boats against the current (逆流而上的船只 / los botes / the boats)
- Dr. T. J. Eckleburg's eyes
- Daisy's voice

No major textual observation was uniquely identified in only one execution. No major observation was missed by any execution.

**Interpretation:** The corpus determines the observation set. Cultural context does not filter which phenomena are noticed at this stage. Observation is the most stable layer of inquiry.

---

### Layer 2: Classification

**Stable across all three executions.**

All three investigators applied the same structural device categories:

| Device Category | English | Spanish | Mandarin |
|---|---|---|---|
| Metaphor | Green light, clock, water, road, boats | Green light, clock, water, road, boats | 隐喻 — same elements |
| Symbol | Eckleburg's eyes | Eckleburg's eyes | 象征 — Eckleburg's eyes |
| Narrative Device | Weather, heat | Weather, heat | 叙事手法 — weather, heat |

The Mandarin essay explicitly articulates the principle driving this classification: "隐喻不仅具有象征意义，而且能够不断参与整个小说意义系统的建构" (A metaphor not only has symbolic meaning but continuously participates in the construction of the novel's entire meaning system). This is a more explicit statement of the same distinction used in the English and Spanish executions.

**Interpretation:** Literary device classification is stable across cultural contexts. The same elements receive the same structural designations regardless of the investigator's cultural frame.

---

### Layer 3: Question Selection

**Diverged across all three executions.**

Three distinct governing questions, none present in the text:

1. *English:* Can an interpretation become more real to someone than reality itself?
2. *Spanish:* Can a person ever separate themselves from their history?
3. *Mandarin:* Can a person refuse the changes of reality and still demand that reality continue according to their wishes?

These questions are not contradictory. They are complementary decompositions:

- **English** identifies *what* the failure is: cognitive loyalty to a fixed interpretation over evidence
- **Spanish** identifies *the relational context*: identity is continuous with history, not separable from it
- **Mandarin** identifies *the process failure*: refusal to adapt one's understanding as reality changes

Each question opens a different corridor into the same building. The corpus supports all three. No execution forced the text into a question it could not sustain.

**Interpretation:** Question selection is the primary culture-adaptive variable. Cultural context does not alter what the investigator sees (observations, classifications). It alters what the investigator asks.

---

### Layer 4: Evidence Weighting

**Downstream from question selection in all three executions.**

Once the governing question was set, each execution reallocated attention within the same observation set:

| Observation | English Weight | Spanish Weight | Mandarin Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Green light | Future/hope/interpretation | Unreachable dream/estrangement | Reality that exists independently of desire |
| Clock | Time cannot be reversed | Cannot restore the past | Attempt to control time is attempt to control reality |
| Water | Separation | Impassable distance from origin | Continuous gap between person and reality |
| Boats against current | Individual fate, futility | Struggle against history's pull | Universal — navigation requires continuous adjustment |
| Nick | Background narrator | Contrast figure | Central exemplar of adaptive capacity |
| Wilson | Minor victim | Social casualty | Catastrophic failure to adapt — system collapse |

No execution invented evidence. All reallocated salience within the same set.

**Interpretation:** Evidence weighting is not determined by the corpus alone or by cultural context alone. It is the interaction product of the corpus and the governing question. Change the question, change the weighting. Keep the corpus.

---

### Layer 5: Compression

**Distinct across all three; non-contradictory.**

| Experiment | Language | Central Compression |
|---|---|---|
| 001 | English | "Reality corrects interpretation." |
| 002 | Spanish | "History remains active." |
| 003 | Mandarin | "Maturity is continuous adaptation, not control." |

These are not three different claims about three different novels. They are three different summaries of the same evidence under three different governing questions. They do not contradict. They are complementary: the English compression names the relationship between mind and evidence; the Spanish names the temporality of that evidence; the Mandarin names the capacity required to respond well.

The conjunction of all three produces a statement no single execution reached:

> *An interpretation that refuses to adapt to changing reality (English) will remain continuous with an unrevisable past (Spanish) because the investigator has not yet learned to update their understanding as reality changes (Mandarin).*

This is a richer claim than any single execution generated.

## The Meta-Observation: Experiment 003 Describes the Architecture

The Mandarin essay explicitly describes its own methodology in Section 二 (Research Methodology):

> **观察 → 分类 → 问题生成 → 收集证据 → 修正假设 → 再次验证**  
> *(Observation → Classification → Question Generation → Evidence Collection → Hypothesis Revision → Re-verification)*

And its governing epistemological principle:

> **文本先于解释，证据先于理论，修正先于结论。**  
> *Text precedes interpretation, evidence precedes theory, revision precedes conclusion.*

This is the Hermeneia pipeline — not cited from Hermeneia, not borrowed from software documentation, but derived independently within the investigation itself, from first principles.

The Mandarin investigator also notes that the initial governing hypothesis ("harmony") was revised when it failed to simultaneously explain the differences between Gatsby, Nick, and Wilson. The governing concept was revised to "continuous adaptation" — and only then did the evidence become coherent.

This is the Architect's function: not to plan an output, but to reconstruct the understanding that the evidence actually supports, including revising the hypothesis when evidence resists it.

The essay demonstrates, from within itself, that the cognitive architecture Hermeneia implements is not an invention of software engineering. It is a description of how disciplined inquiry actually works.

## Implications for the Research Program

### What three data points establish

The hypothesis that "question selection is the primary culture-adaptive variable" is now supported by three executions across three distinct cultural and linguistic contexts. The pattern has been observed; it has not been contradicted; it has held under a third test.

The threshold for calling this an **Established Pattern** (observed across multiple axes — language AND domain) has not been met. That requires domain experiments (Genesis, Supreme Court opinion, etc.) to confirm the same pattern outside of literary analysis.

### What three data points suggest about Hermeneia

If the observations are stable but questions diverge, then the Hermeneia pipeline is not introducing the divergence — it is revealing it. The same pipeline, the same corpus, three investigators, three governing questions. The divergence was in the investigators, not the system.

This means Hermeneia's pipeline is functioning as designed: separating observation (stable, corpus-determined) from question selection (variable, investigator-determined) makes the source of divergence visible. Without that separation, the divergence would be hidden inside a single generative output.

The architecture makes the evolution of understanding inspectable — and the three-experiment comparison is the first demonstration of what that inspection reveals.

### What this suggests about the fractal property

The Mandarin essay's explicit derivation of the pipeline from first principles within the investigation raises a specific question: if a disciplined investigator working on any corpus will naturally discover the same cognitive sequence (observe → classify → generate questions → gather evidence → revise → verify), then the pipeline is not a workflow imposed by software. It is a description of how understanding develops.

This would mean the architecture generalizes beyond Hermeneia. It would mean the same sequence applies wherever disciplined inquiry is practiced — in literary analysis, in legal reasoning, in scientific investigation, in organizational learning.

That remains a **Research Hypothesis** — stated here because three experiments have created the conditions under which it becomes testable.
