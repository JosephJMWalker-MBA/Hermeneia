# Human Stewardship

Hermeneia increases human interpretation rather than replacing it.

---

## Stewardship as Ecological Role

Stewardship in PUA is not a job title or a permission level. It is a relational property arising between a participant, the artifacts they have contributed, and the ecology they participate in — recognized through repeated cycles of observation, preservation, correction, and inheritance.

A steward does not possess stewardship the way a person holds a credential. Stewardship is something the ecology recognizes in a participant based on what the lineage reveals.

## Three Kinds of Stewardship

These must never be conflated:

**Declared Stewardship** — credentials asserted by the steward or an institution. MD, PhD, FAA Part 107, CPA, peer-reviewed publication count. Real information. Stored as provenance. Unverified by the system.

**Derived Stewardship** — attributes computed directly from the steward's participation in the corpus. Observations contributed, interpretations created, self-revisions made, interpretations corroborated by independent evidence, contradictions resolved, stewardship cycles completed. Unfakeable. The ecology observes participation and computes stewardship from lineage.

**Conferred Stewardship** — explicit governance roles granted by the ecology itself. Invited reviewer, ratification signer, editorial board member, constitutional steward. These are procedural, not epistemic. A conferred role grants the authority to initiate a stewardship cycle; it does not grant semantic standing to the interpretations that steward produces.

## Invariant XIII

The semantic standing of any artifact — Observation, Interpretation, Perspective, Narrative Blueprint, Architect Plan, or Validation Report — shall never be increased or decreased solely because of the institutional credentials, reputation, identity, or social status of its steward.

Credentials are context, not evidence. Expertise is signal, not proof. The system preserves minority, dissenting, and novice interpretations alongside expert interpretations when supported by provenance. Semantic commitment remains grounded in observations, explicit reasoning, and validation artifacts.

This invariant protects the architecture from two equal and opposite failures:

*Credentialism* produces a system where Semmelweis never exists — where institutional consensus silences the correct dissent before it can be heard.

*False egalitarianism* erases the signal that provenance provides — treating an interpretation backed by 2,400 corroborated observations the same as a guess backed by zero.

The architecture must preserve both artifacts while exposing their provenance honestly. The human steward reads the lineage and exercises judgment. The system preserves the lineage without pre-weighting it.

## Emergent Authority

Authority in PUA is not a prerequisite for participation. It is a byproduct of sustained, validated participation.

Consider two stewards:

**Steward A**: PhD, 2 papers, 10 observations, 5 interpretations.

**Steward B**: No degree, 35 years studying tides, 18,000 observations, 2,400 interpretations, 97% later corroborated, frequent self-correction, interpretations still being inherited by future Architect Plans.

The ecology can answer the question of authority without consulting a résumé. It reads the lineage.

This is what PUA means by *validated authority*: the emergent property of long-term stewardship demonstrated through repeated cycles of observation, interpretation, correction, corroboration, preservation, and inheritance. It can only emerge through time. It cannot be manufactured overnight because the ecology evaluates relationships, not counts.

An observation without citations, corroboration, downstream interpretations, or blueprint inheritance is visible as isolated — regardless of how many such observations exist. The relational structure of the corpus exposes shallow activity without requiring an explicit penalty mechanism.

## Stewardship Legacy

The accumulated record of contributions that continue to demonstrate semantic validity, preserve provenance, survive criticism, and remain useful across successive stewardship cycles.

This is what Semmelweis eventually accumulated. At the time of his institutional rejection, his derived stewardship existed in the corpus. His validated authority emerged retrospectively, when the lineage was re-examined. A system that preserves lineage instead of freezing consensus eventually converges toward recognizing contributions the original consensus rejected.

Authority becomes retrospective evidence, not merely contemporaneous status.

## The Recursive Loop

```
Observation
↓
Contribution
↓
Stewardship
↓
Validation
↓
Inheritance
↓
Authority
↓
Better Stewardship
↓
Better Observations
↓
...
```

A healthy ecology does not merely preserve understanding. It continually learns who its best preservers are — and does so by remembering what they have actually preserved.
