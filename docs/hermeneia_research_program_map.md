**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-06-25  
**Status:** Ratified

## Purpose

This document defines the constitutional role of every major artifact in the Hermeneia research program. It answers:

> What is each document *for*, what knowledge does it produce, and how does it relate to the others?

Its purpose is to prevent role drift, duplication, and conceptual confusion as the body of work grows. Every future document should be able to locate itself on this map before it earns its place in the literature.

---

## The Core Distinction

Hermeneia is simultaneously three things:

| Layer | Primary Question | Nature |
|---|---|---|
| **Methodology** | How does understanding evolve? | Research program |
| **Architecture** | How do we make that evolution inspectable and governable? | Constitutional framework |
| **Instrument** | How do we observe and measure the evolution of understanding? | Scientific tool |

The documents below serve different layers of the same program.

## Structure of the Research Program

The program is not a flat collection of papers. It has a hierarchy in which each level answers a question the level above it raises.

```
                     Blueprint 000001
                   (Constitutional Intent)
                            │
                            │ governs
                            ▼
         Toward an Ecology of Intelligence
              (Foundational Theory)
                            │
                            │ motivates
                            ▼
           Hermeneia White Paper ◄─── Writing Protocol
                (Methodology)          (Operational
                            │           Procedure)
                            │ enables
                            ▼
          Semantic Contract Fulfillment
               (Measurement)
                            │
                            │ grounds
                            ▼
                      Applications
             Education · Research · AI · Law · Science

                                         Pitch Deck
                                      (Translation &
                                         Adoption)
```

**Reading the diagram:**

- Blueprint 000001 governs the White Paper — it is the constitutional intent from which the paper is rendered.
- Ecology of Intelligence supplies the foundational question: what is intelligence, and how does it persist? That question motivates the Hermeneia methodology.
- The White Paper answers how to study the evolution of understanding — it is the methodological core.
- SCF answers how to measure whether communication preserved understanding — it is the measurement layer that Hermeneia makes possible.
- The Writing Protocol sits beside the White Paper: it is the methodology applied to its own production.
- The Pitch Deck stands apart. It translates the research program for audiences who need adoption narrative, not methodological depth.

This is not a flat ecosystem. It is a succession: Foundational Theory → Methodology → Measurement → Applications.

## Key Relationships

**Blueprint 000001 → White Paper**  
The Blueprint is the canonical artifact. The White Paper is one rendering of it. Future investigators may render different papers — more mathematical, more philosophical, more educational, more legal — from the same Blueprint, and all faithful renderings remain valid.

**Ecology of Intelligence → Hermeneia**  
Ecology asks: what is intelligence? Hermeneia asks: how does understanding evolve? These are successive levels of abstraction, not siblings. Hermeneia's methodology presupposes that understanding is worth studying; Ecology supplies the theoretical account of why.

**Hermeneia → SCF**  
Hermeneia makes the stages of inquiry inspectable. SCF asks whether a specific stage — communication — preserved the understanding that preceded it. SCF is only possible because Hermeneia separates communication from reconstruction. That separation is not a software design choice. It is the prerequisite for measurement.

**White Paper + Writing Protocol + Provenance Record**  
These three form a single demonstration: the White Paper is the claim; the Writing Protocol is the methodology by which it was produced; the Provenance Record is the evidence that the methodology was actually applied. The claim, the method, and the proof of method are distinct artifacts.

**Pitch Deck**  
The Pitch Deck is the only Tier 2 document. Its job is translation and adoption narrative. It should not be edited to add methodological depth. Depth belongs in the White Paper. The Pitch Deck has a different mission: make the program understandable to people who will not read the White Paper first.

## Role of This Map

This document is not a paper. It does not make claims about intelligence, methodology, or measurement.

It answers one question: what are we looking at?

A researcher encountering Hermeneia for the first time should read this document before reading any other. It will tell them which document answers which question, and why each document exists in relation to the others.

---

## Governance Protocol

Any new document proposed for the Hermeneia research program must first answer:

1. Which cell in this map does it occupy?
2. What knowledge does it produce that no existing document produces?
3. Which existing documents does it depend on, and which depend on it?

If these questions cannot be answered, the document is not ready to be written.

Future revisions to this map require explicit ratification.
