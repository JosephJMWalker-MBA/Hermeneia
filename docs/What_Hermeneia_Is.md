# What Hermeneia Is

**Status:** PROJECT IDENTITY STATEMENT
**Authority:** Interpretive orientation only
**Implementation status:** DOCUMENTATION ONLY

---

Hermeneia is an operating environment for the disciplined evolution of understanding.

It is not primarily an AI. It is not a document analyzer. It is not a chatbot.

It is the environment where inquiry itself has explicit cognitive roles,
governance, evidence, and accountability.

Hermeneia preserves the evolution of understanding by separating and governing
the cognitive responsibilities through which understanding develops.

The confirmed cognitive responsibilities are:

- **Discovery** — Explorer surfaces candidate interpretations from evidence
- **Reconstruction** — Architect formalizes semantic obligations from understanding
- **Communication** — Artist realizes understanding in a chosen expressive form
- **Verification** — Critic evaluates whether expression preserved meaning
- **Governance** — Steward exercises judgment that cannot be reduced to computation

A candidate responsibility under investigation:

- **Attention** — Witness records what caught human notice before interpretation begins

The candidate status matters constitutionally. Hermeneia does not create
ontology until repeated use proves it deserves to exist. The Witness
responsibility is architecturally compelling and supported by experimental
observation, but requires practice before earning canonical status.

Those are not software modules. They are cognitive responsibilities — and they
are ordered. Each is the prerequisite for the next. Separating them makes
failures diagnosable. Preserving them makes understanding auditable.

---

Hermeneia also distinguishes between what must endure and what may be
regenerated.

Everything else follows from this distinction.

Irreducible epistemic objects are preserved with immutable ancestry and
explicit provenance. Computational functions, analytical methods, user
interfaces, and summaries are treated as disposable infrastructure that can be
regenerated from canonical knowledge without loss.

This distinction is expressed by two orienting principles:

> **Conservation of Ontology:** No new ontological object shall be introduced
> if it can be derived from existing constitutional objects through established
> architectural patterns.

> **Regeneration Principle:** Persist irreducible truth. Regenerate everything
> else.

Accordingly, Hermeneia recognizes a small set of primitive architectural
categories:

- **Canonical Objects** - preserved epistemic artifacts whose identity and
  lineage matter.
- **Functions** - deterministic computations that transform canonical inputs
  into canonical outputs.
- **Projections** - disposable views optimized for particular audiences or
  tasks.
- **Derived Artifacts** - regenerable products computed from canonical
  objects.
- **Human Judgments** - stewardship, witnessing, and ratification that cannot
  be reduced to computation.

Everything else in the architecture must derive from these primitives or
justify constitutional review.

Hermeneia deliberately separates constitutional law, engineering, and science.

The **Constitution** defines what exists.

The **Engineering Layer** defines how understanding is preserved.

The **Scientific Layer** explains why preservation succeeds or fails.

Scientific analysis may draw on information theory, linguistics, cognitive
science, and future disciplines without altering the constitutional substrate.

This separation allows the engineering to improve while the epistemic
foundation remains stable.

Understanding is treated not as a property of an individual mind or model, but
as a persistent relationship between evidence, interpretation, expression,
evaluation, and stewardship capable of outliving any single participant.

Hermeneia is provider-independent by design. Models, providers, interfaces,
algorithms, and analytical theories may evolve or be replaced. Canonical
understanding and its lineage remain.

Its goal is simple:

> **To make understanding inspectable, corrigible, transmissible, and
> preservable across languages, expressive forms, providers, institutions, and
> generations.**

Its ambition is not to preserve models.

Its ambition is not to preserve prompts.

Its ambition is not to preserve interfaces.

Its ambition is to preserve understanding.

## Three Layers

**Layer 1 — Constitutional Infrastructure**

The substrate that makes inquiry trustworthy.

- Immutable lineage and provenance
- Stewardship and ratification
- Semantic obligations and evaluation functions
- End-to-end traceability
- Multi-profile rendering and translation
- Constitutional proofs

This layer answers: *Can we trust the system?*

**Layer 2 — Cognitive Architecture**

The separation of cognitive responsibilities across pipeline roles.

```
Explorer       discovers candidate interpretations
Architect      reconstructs semantic obligations
Artist         communicates in expressive form
Critic         verifies that expression preserved meaning
[Witness]      candidate: records human attention before interpretation begins
```

This layer answers: *Can meaning survive different expressions?*

**Layer 3 — Research Governance**

The framework that governs inquiry itself.

```
Execution Contract
    ↓
Research Session
    ↓
Investigation
    ↓
Interpretation
```

This layer governs objective, completion, protocol revision, and
reproducibility — not knowledge, but research.

---

Hermeneia is not primarily an AI framework. It is an operating environment
for disciplined inquiry, where AI occupies one cognitive role among many,
and where that role is constitutionally bounded, evaluated, and accountable.
