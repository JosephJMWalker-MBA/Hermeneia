# Vision

Hermeneia seeks to become a durable operating system for human
understanding.

Its purpose is to preserve observations, organize perspectives, and
cultivate dialogue across generations.

Hermeneia treats legacy as more than a collection of finished works.

A legacy is an investigation whose evidence trail is complete enough that
another steward can continue it responsibly.

Hermeneia asks how understanding can be responsibly transmitted across time:
not by preserving conclusions alone, but by preserving the conditions under
which interpretation can continue.

The goal is not to freeze conclusions. The goal is to preserve the means by
which truth was sought, tested, revised, criticized, and handed forward.
